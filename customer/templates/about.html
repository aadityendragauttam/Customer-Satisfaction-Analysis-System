About Us

"Customer Satisfaction Predictor" conjures imagery of resilience and protection, suggesting a machine learning project that repurposes raw passenger data and flight metrics into powerful vehicles for improving the airline experience. The term "shell" evokes notions of security and operational safety, hinting at the idea of predictive analytics as a protective cocoon for an airline's reputation and passenger loyalty. This concept carries a symbolic weight, implying a focus on sustainability, resourcefulness, and data-driven trust. By breathing new life into historical flight data—such as delays, cleanliness, and in-flight service ratings—this project embodies the principles of continuous service improvement and passenger churn reduction. Moreover, the name suggests a sense of belonging and community, as a seamless travel experience fosters a feeling of a global home and interconnectedness between airlines and travelers. Through this initiative, aviation data is transformed into actionable insights, inviting airlines to participate in a collective effort to build a more reliable, responsive, and satisfying passenger journey.
